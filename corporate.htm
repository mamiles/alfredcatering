At Your Service Catering and Events can provide for a full range of corporate events such as open houses, grand openings, galas, picnics, award luncheons, retirement parties and holiday events.  Our goal is to make your and your company look good with little to no effort on your part.

As a full service catering and event planning company, we are completely equipped to handle all aspects of your event.  We can provide tables, chairs, linens, rentals, entertainment, decorations, props, mobile bars or even a margarita machine. You can count on us to handle your needs. We also provide full service premium bars, fine wines, beer and a variety of non-alcoholic beverages.   Our menus vary from simple to elaborately themed events, and our team of professionals will be happy to handle all the details of your events. We can turn your venue into the right environment or provide a listing of venues to host your event. We take great pride in our quality service and the presentation of our buffets. Our motto is “Be A Guest At Your Own Party”, and we’ve been helping people do just that since 1992.
