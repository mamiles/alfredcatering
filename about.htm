At Your Service Catering and Events can fulfill your party needs in every way. We do this by asking the right questions and listening carefully to your responses, to anticipate your unique needs and desires for your occasion. At Your Service Catering and Events, formerly Alfred’s Catering, is constructed of a team of seasoned professionals with over thirty years of experience in the industry. We understand every aspect of catering and event services, including site selection, entertainment planning, and vendor & event management.
